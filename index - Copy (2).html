Research

Leverage structure of knowledge to learn its interaction with the unknown.

Our research lies at the interface of human genetics, computational statistics, and data science. 
Current research focuses on understanding diverse behaviors of cells using machine learning, network 
science and dynamical system analysis. We develop analytical frameworks to study single-cell 
transcriptome data from various types of cells. We also study the genetic basis of phenotypic 
variability or randomness in the human population and develop computational tools to identify 
genetic variants that control complex traits and determine the susceptibility of genetic disorders.
